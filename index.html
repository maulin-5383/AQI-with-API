Air Pollution Scale

Good:

The air quality is cosidered satisfactory, and air pollution poses little or no threat.

Moderate

Air quality is acceptable; however for some air pollutants there maybe moderate health concern for very small number of people who are unusually sensitive to air pollution.

Unhealthy to sensitive groups

Members of sensitive group might be affected. General public are not affected by such air pollution.
→ If you have heart or lung disease, if you are an older adult, or if you have children, talk with your doctor in advance about when and whether you should leave the area or move to a location with better indoor air quality for a temporary period of time.
→ Don't vaccum the place because that will stire up the particles inside your house.

Unhealthy

Everyone may begin to experience health effect. People of sensitive group might have some more serious effects than others. In this case there are certain precaution you need to take. They are:
⨷ A face mask will protect you from air pollution, as long as it is the right type of mask. Make sure your mask is rated N95 and fit snugly over your nose and mouth
⨷ Keep your activity level low. Avoid activities that make you breathe faster or more deeply. Instead just watch TV or read books.

Hazardous

♦ If the air quality is in very unhealthy or hazardous range, chances are an emergency alert will be issued by media outlets, weather apps and more.
♦ Stay indoors and at a place having high efficiency air filters.
♦ If you cannot buy filters for your entire house, create a clean room for sleeping. A room with minmum windows and doors will do the work.
